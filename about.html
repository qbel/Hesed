History

Take A Look at How We Begin

As an academic institution, Hesed Academy, a K-12 school, offers exceptional teaching services in a conducive and standard learning environment. As a Christian Private school, it is result driven, anchor is biblical truth, with sound academic pedigree and is very affordable fee. Hesed Academy operates with a learner-centered philosophy of education, i.e., our students’ overall best interest always remains the primary motivation for what we do. We are guided by our Christian values and professional ethics. And, we adhere to the local education regulations and corporate business laws. Hesed Academy is wholly owned by Hesed Resource & Development Foundation (HESED RDF), a non-profit faith-based corporation that is focused on human capital development and societal transformation.
